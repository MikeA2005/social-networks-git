Andres Barrera

What will you do, when you finish school?

When I finish school I will study a professional career at the university that has to do with investments and start investing and generating more profits.

Where will you live if you have to move? Why?

I would live in the same neighborhood where I grew up, since it is the place where I grew up, the most significant experiences of my life I have lived in that neighborhood, also my grandparents have lived there since I was little, I would like to live in that neighborhood always.

If you had the opportunity to travel abroad, where would you go? Why?

I would travel to the United States, to get to know different cultures, gastronomic dishes, etc. Also to have more job opportunities, since it is a developed country.

If you travelled to the past, what would you change?

I would change several things that I should not have done, and some that may affect the future.
