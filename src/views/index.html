Social Networks

Social networks are social structures formed on the web by a set of people or organizations that relate to each other based on common interests or values.

What are social networks?

Social networks, in the digital world, are applications that operate in different fields, but in each one they share the same purpose, to exchange information.

Advantages | disadvantages

In social networks we can find absolutely everything, with multiple purposes. And it is precisely this variety that is responsible for having both pros and cons:

  • Advantages
  • Disadvantages

Advantages

  • Global communication:with social networks there are no limits in connection
  • Viralization:because everything is interconnected we can viralize publications a little easier.
  • Promotion:through these we can promote our company or business, a more affordable form of marketing for all classes.
  • Entertainment:networks entertain users, and thanks to their algorithms, networks recommend the content with which you have the most affinity.

Disadvantages

  • Dangerous algorithms:what we mentioned as previously on the other hand is counterproductive, because if you see the publications that you like and not those that you do not like, a different thought can make you intolerant.
  • Harassment:harassment has always existed but with social networks it has increased, since you can create false profiles.
  • Social pressure:through social networks, people idealize various stereotypes of perfect people, many people feel anguish due to this pressure to gain more likes and followers.
  • Communication:communication and the media are very close; each person can publish news and we can frequently find false news.
